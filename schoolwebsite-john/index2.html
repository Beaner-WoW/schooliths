Parent Coordinator

Dear Parents and Guardians, Here’s to a new school year and welcome to Information Technology High school! My name is Judy Boror and I am your new parent coordinator. I am here to provide parents access to all Info Tech High School matters. Info Tech High School is an excellent school environment for your children as the staff is very dedicated and committed to helping students grow to achieve their full academic and personal potential. As parent coordinator, my main objective is to encourage parent involvement. You, as a parent or guardian, have the right to voice concerns, needs and ideas pertaining to your child’s education and well-being while in school. We welcome all of your suggestions and points of view, so that together, we can help our children thrive. Your involvement in your child’s education and support are essential to the success that lies ahead. Parents and guardians are kept informed of events and activities by methods such as monthly Parent Association meetings, workshops, emails, phone messenger calls, and our school website https://infotechhs.net. Another one of our highly effective communication systems is JupiterEd. JupiterEd is a website that ITHS uses to keep you informed of your child’s progress. You can log in anytime to check your child’s grades, attendance, missing work, test scores, and communicate with teachers and staff. In order to receive your username and password, please contact me. I am located in room 117, Monday to Friday, from 7:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. I can be reached at 718-937-4270 ext. 1171 and by email at Jboror2@schools.nyc.gov. My door is always open and no appointment is necessary. I will be glad to help you with any questions, concerns or issues that you may have. I look forward to meeting you and working together to make the upcoming school year successful for all! Sincerely, Ms. Judy Boror Parent Coordinator
